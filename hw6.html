Transformed Responce Variable

plot(dataBoston$lstat, log(dataBoston$crim), xlab = "percentage lower status of the population",
     ylab ="log per capita crime rate by town" )

There exist a positive relationship between per capita crime rate by town and percentage lower status of the population and there is a low variation in the variables as compared to the other plot. The strength of the relationship is stronger than what exists without the transposed response variable.
